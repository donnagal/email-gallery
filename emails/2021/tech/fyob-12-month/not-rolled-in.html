It’s easy to round up all
your super and save

If you have multiple super accounts, with super savings all over the place, you could really benefit from bringing all your accounts together in one place, with Cbus. The benefits are clear:

 

  
 

Save money on fees.

One super fund means you only pay
one set of fees, potentially saving
you hundreds and even thousands of dollars over the years.

  
 
 
 

Easier to manage.

One fund is easier to keep track
of, meaning you can focus on
your investment strategy for
the future.

  
 
 
 

Reduce paperwork.

Nobody likes dealing with more paperwork than they need to.
Having only one fund means
only one set of paperwork.

Please note, there are some important things to consider before you combine your super. Visit: https://www.cbussuper.com.au/super/consolidate-or-find-my-super
