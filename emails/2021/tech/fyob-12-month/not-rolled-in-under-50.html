If you’re worried you won’t have enough money when you retire, think about making extra contributions to your super account today. It’s never too late to start. Even small amounts can make a big difference to your lifestyle later on.

One easy way to make extra contributions is through Salary Sacrifice.

Please note, limits apply to these contributions. Click here for more information.

 
 

The benefits of salary sacrifice

Lower tax rate

you make super contributions through a salary sacrifice, these contributions are taxed at a maximum rate of 15%.

When super is paid from your pre-tax salary, your taxable income is lowered. And the higher your tax bracket, the less tax you pay on the money that’s salary sacrificed to super.

  
 

Super growth

Over time, you’ll enjoy the benefit of compounding returns in your super fund. And as your account grows over time you’ll see how even a little extra contribution now, can mean a lot to your savings at retirement.
