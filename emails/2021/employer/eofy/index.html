Changes to super laws
could impact your employees

The Government wants to change super laws that could see workers new to the industry ‘stapled’ to a fund that won’t give them insurance that covers dangerous occupations (stapling would link a worker to their first super account, not the one they need in their current job).

Cbus is concerned that proposed new changes to super laws could mean your workers are left without adequate insurance. There are things you can do to help.
