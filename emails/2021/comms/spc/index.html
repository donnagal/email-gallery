How Cbus is helping members collect unpaid super.

Our top priority is making sure our members have enough money to live on in retirement. Unpaid super is a problem which leaves many of our members with much less than they’re entitled to.

If you work in the building, construction and allied sector, you can ask to have the status of your super contributions checked by a union delegate or official. It’s an easy way for our members to take control of their super contributions. We provide a limited amount of personal member information to union delegates or official to get their help in checking and recovering unpaid super.

 
 

What information do we share?

  • Name
  • Application form has been signed
  • SG Contribution Amounts received in last 6 months
  • Date SG contributions Received and the Time periods they are paid for
  • If Salary Sacrifice Payment Received (no amount shown)
  • Employer Name and ABN

What if you don't opt out?

If you do not opt out then we will assume that you consent to us providing the above types of information to union officials and delegates.

However, if you change your mind you can opt out later using the options available below. Please note that it can take up to 5 business days to manage this request.

 
 

Other ways to Opt-out:

  1. Download the Cbus' unpaid super services consent form
  2. Call us 1300 361 784 from 8:30am to 6pm (AEST/AEDT), Monday to Friday
  3. In person with a Coordinator
 
 

If you would like further information about our unpaid super recovery work, we have further information available on our website.

If you have any other questions, then please call us on 1300 361 784 from 8:30am to 6pm (AEST/AEDT), Monday to Friday.
