If you’ve changed jobs, you may have a number of different super accounts. If so, it’s likely you’re paying more fees than you need to.

By combining your super into one account you could:

•

save money by only paying one set of fees

•

gain control by knowing exactly how much you have and how it’s growing

  

•

simplify your paperwork with just one statement.

  

Ready, set, roll

Selecting the right super fund is an important decision so make sure you take some time to decide. If you need help, you can call Cbus to talk through your options. When you are ready you can combine in just a few steps.

 
 
STEP

1

If you know your other super account details, log in to your account and select ‘I have my other super account details’

 
STEP

2

Type the name of your fund into the ‘Search for super fund’ field and then select the matching fund.

 
STEP

3

Enter your Member/Account number and the amount you want to transfer, agree to the disclaimer, and click ‘Rollover to Cbus.’

 
Log in to get started
