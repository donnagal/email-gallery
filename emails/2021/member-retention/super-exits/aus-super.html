Cbus is standing up for members in the construction industry

Cbus is concerned about the proposed Government plan to ‘staple’ members to their first fund for their working life – the plan could see workers stapled to a Fund with unsuitable insurance, unless you choose another. The fund that might have worked for you in your first job when you worked in a pub or similar, may not have the insurance you need as a building and construction worker. Cbus is fighting for the proposed super laws to be changed so they recognise and cater for workers in dangerous occupations, like building and construction.

Cbus is fighting so that workers in our industry are:

  • Not stapled to a fund with unsuitable insurance options
  • Not paying for insurance with other funds that won’t cover them.
  • Not putting themselves and their familes at risk with no protection.
