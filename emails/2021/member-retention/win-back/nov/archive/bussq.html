We keep fees low

We’re an industry fund, so we keep all fees as low as possible. We only charge the administration and investment fees needed to run your account. Below, you can compare our fees for the Growth (MySuper) investment option against BUSSQ and other funds.

The main difference between a retail super fund and an industry super fund like Cbus is how their profits are managed. Retail super funds are usually run by financial institutions, like banks and investment companies which have a responsibility to shareholders.

Industry super funds are not-for-profit organisations which means profits are returned to members, not shareholders.

We protect high risk workers

Most funds exclude cover for dangerous occupations, but Cbus doesn’t. We offer tailored insurance options for members working with heavy machinery and from heights, because we understand the risks of working in the building, construction and allied industries.

If this sounds like you, Cbus may be one of the few options available for you to obtain adequate insurance cover.
