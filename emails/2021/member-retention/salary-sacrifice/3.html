How Salary Sacrifice works

Salary Sacrifice is the tax-smart way to boost your super. Simply instruct your employer to direct a small amount of your pre-tax income into your super account. Because your taxable income is lower, you could pay less tax, plus you have more super. Itâ€™s well worth considering if your income is over $45,000.
