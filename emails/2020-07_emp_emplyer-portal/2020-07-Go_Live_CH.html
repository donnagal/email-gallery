Keep an eye out for your activation email

You will receive an email with a link to activate Employer Online within 2 weeks after Employer Online goes live – so keep an eye on your inbox. Your activation link will be valid for 24 hours.

If you lose your activation email, or if your activation link expires, don’t worry - you will still be able to activate your account directly from the Employer Online login screen by clicking on Don’t know your username.

Last chance to update your details

If you haven’t done so already, you have until 7pm Wednesday, 5th August to log into Cbus Clearing House to update the mobile number and email address for all of your business contacts. These details will be key to activating Employer Online when it’s ready.
