Is your insurance cover up to date?

When life changes, your insurance cover needs to change with you.

  • Dangerous Occupation Exception.

    It's over a year since a policy to protect workers in hazardous occupations has been in operation...

    This policy result can be attributed to Cbus' advocacy in Parliament on behalf of our members. The Dangerous Occupation Exception has allowed over 144,000 Cbus Members to obtain or retain vital insurance.

  • How do I nominate beneficiaries?

    Beneficiaries are the people you elect to receive your super, and any insurance benefits, when you die…

    Most people think their superannuation automatically becomes part of their estate when they die. But it doesn’t. Cbus has to follow super and tax laws to decide who is eligible to receive your superannuation and any insurance payments (i.e. your beneficiaries).

    For more about nominating beneficiaries watch our videos explaining binding nominations.

    Did you know when you change jobs you can remain with Cbus? This way you can still keep insurance designed for people in the building and construction industry...
