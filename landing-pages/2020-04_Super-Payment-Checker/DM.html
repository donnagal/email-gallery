Super Payment Checker Opt-out

Opt-out below

If you do not want your super details viewed by third parties such as Delegates or Union Officials including; Name, Year of Birth, Membership Number, Insurance Category, SG Contribution Amount, Date payment Received and Time period paid for, Salary Sacrifice Payment, Employer Name and ABN to be displayed in the Super Payment Checker, you can simply opt-out.

It’s important to note that if you choose to opt-out, it can take up to 5 business days (TBC) to administer this request.

It’s important to note that on-site card checking will negate this opt-out for the Super Payment Checker App. The opt-out however will still be valid for the Super Payment Checker Process.

You can use this form to tell us that you want to opt-out of having your personal details listed in the Super Payment Checker App and Super Payment Checker Process.
